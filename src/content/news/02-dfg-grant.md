---
title: "New grant awarded by DFG for Ethical AI Analysis"
date: 2024-09-28
---
We are delighted to announce that the DFG (Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft) has awarded a new research grant to the WüNLP Chair for the project "Measuring and Mitigating Bias in Pre-trained Language Models." The project will run for three years starting in early 2025.
